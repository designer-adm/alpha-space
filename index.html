About Us

Established in 1988, Alpha Space Solutions has been offering wide range of services in the field of architecture, interior/exterior designing.We have more than 30 years of experience in the Construction Industry.Whether designing residential or commercial spaces, Alpha has built a reputation on achieving high quality results for a broad clientele.One-Roof Solution for the total construction industry involving Site Survey, Development Architecture, Designing, Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing, Fire, Interior Works etc. We are ably supported by an efficient team which include professional designers and supervisors. We strive to give our best services in this domain. We, Alpha Space Solutions Pvt Ltd, situated at Vasant Kunj, Delhi, believe, good design is produced from careful study and research, combined with technical knowledge and artistic judgment. It's our aim to create spaces where people & activities thrive. Our team is capable of handling all the projects from the initial concept till the actual completion of the whole project.
